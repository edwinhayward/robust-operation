### Contribution Guidelines

This project was initially developed with the assistance of AI and is maintained with a focus on stability and clarity. I welcome contributions, but to maintain the quality of the codebase, all Pull Requests **must** adhere to the following:

1.  **Provide a Clear Justification:** Explain the problem you are solving. Why is your change necessary? What bug does it fix? What edge case does it handle? Link to an existing Issue if possible.
2.  **Prove it Works:** Describe how you tested your change. While I don't have a formal test suite yet, you must be able to explain the steps to reproduce the problem and verify your fix.
3.  **Maintain Code Quality:** Your code must match the existing style and include clear JSDoc comments for any new logic.
4.  **The Burden of Proof is on You:** The maintainer unfortunately has limited time to vet contributions. If your explanation is unclear or your change is not obviously beneficial and safe, it will likely be closed.

By submitting a Pull Request, you agree that your contribution is your own original work and that you have the right to license it under the project's MIT License.

Thank you for your consideration.
